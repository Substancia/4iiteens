About Us




4IITEENS is a highly competent institute for perfect coaching with a sole motive to guide students to get admission in top best Dream colleges/universities and world best colleges.

Top three academicians from Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry decided to come together for a common purpose to provide a simplified systematic and sustainable coaching for IITJEE.

IITJEE requires a very systematic preparation and it examines your basic concepts its logical aspects and your ability to apply fundamentals to multi-conceptual problems.

4IITEENS has designed an unique approach:

  • 1. Detailed schedule for teaching
  • 2. Teaching basic concepts by taking conceptual approach rather relying on the limited set of formulas
  • 3. Giving practice through DPP, chapterwise assignments
  • 4. Weekly and monthly systematic periodic tests
  • 5. Analysis of Test to helps the students to measure their strength, time management and exam strategy.
  • 6. Doubt clarification sessions
  • 7. Intensive revision Program


WHO: The training in 4IITEENS emphasizes on strategic methods towards approaching students supremely confident of their abilities. 4IITEENS provides the best possible preparation for students which eventually leads to student passing their target scores by healthy margins.

BY WHOME: Finest faculty team in 4IITEENS has an amazing track record by providing student with silver medal in International ChemistryÂ  Olympiad, International Physics Olympiad and students with AIR below 50 in JEE exams.

WHY: 4IITEENS understands the need of coaching to take even the brightest of students to the next level for competitive exams to score up to their full potential and secure admission to the best colleges of their choice.
